# Walkthrough - MediGuard AI (Purple & White Theme)

MediGuard AI is a cross-platform React Native app built using Expo. It targets elderly safety by analyzing medications, flagging high-power warnings, checking active drug-drug interactions, and offering telehealth doctor links.

We have updated the design to use a clean **Purple & White light theme**!

## Summary of Theme Customizations

1. **Brand Colors**: Configured `theme.js` to use royal purple (`#6D28D9`) as the primary brand color, and lavender (`#E8DFFE`) for boundaries.
2. **Readability**: Swapped background to white-lavender (`#FAF8FF`) and typography text to deep plum (`#1E1430`) for maximum visual contrast.
3. **Control Contrast**: Upgraded active solid buttons (Primary buttons, warning banners, chat bubbles, and action buttons) to use crisp white text (`#FFFFFF`) to maintain accessibility guidelines.

## How to Run the App

The Metro bundler is online and listening. 

### 📱 Running on Android (Physical Device)

1. Download the free **Expo Go** app from the Google Play Store on your phone.
2. Connect your phone and computer to the same Wi-Fi network.
3. In the Expo Go app, select the option to **Enter URL manually**.
4. Enter the connection URL:
   👉 **`exp://10.36.105.243:8081`**

### 💻 Running on Web

1. Open your browser and navigate to:
   👉 **[http://localhost:8081](http://localhost:8081)**
2. The browser hot-reloaded the changes and will display the new purple & white theme!
