# 🩺 MediGuard AI – Intelligent Medicine Safety & Drug Interaction Analyzer

## 📌 Overview

MediGuard AI is an AI-powered mobile and web application designed to improve medicine safety. It helps users understand their medicines, identify potential drug interactions, and access reliable medicine information. The application is especially useful for elderly patients and caregivers who manage multiple medications.

## 🚀 Features

* 📷 Scan medicine strips or prescriptions using OCR
* 💊 View medicine details such as uses, dosage, side effects, and precautions
* ⚠️ Detect potential drug interactions with medicines already being taken
* 🤖 AI chatbot for medicine-related questions
* 👨‍⚕️ Find and connect with nearby doctors
* 📋 Maintain medicine history
* 🔒 Secure user authentication

## 📱 How It Works

1. User scans a medicine strip or uploads a medicine image.
2. OCR extracts the medicine name.
3. The app retrieves medicine information from the database.
4. AI checks for possible drug interactions with the user's existing medicines.
5. The app displays the interaction risk level and an easy-to-understand explanation.
6. Users can ask additional questions through the AI chatbot or contact nearby doctors if needed.

## 🎯 Problem Statement

Many people are unaware of medicine side effects and harmful drug interactions, especially when caring for elderly family members. MediGuard AI helps users make informed decisions by providing medicine information and identifying potential interaction risks. It is designed to support users, not replace professional medical advice.
